📋 Health & Safety Legislation Reference
Key legislation applicable to gym and fitness facility operations in England
Legislation What It Requires How We Comply Review Freq. Status
Health & Safety at Work Act 1974 General duty of care to employees and the public. Provide safe systems of work, training, and supervision. Staff inductions, daily checks, incident log Ongoing Active
Management of H&S at Work Regs 1999 Formal written risk assessments required. Must identify significant hazards and record findings. RA module — all activities assessed and stored Annual / on change Active
Provision & Use of Work Equipment Regs 1998 (PUWER) All gym equipment must be suitable, maintained, inspected regularly, and operators trained. Equipment log, weekly PUWER checks, service records Weekly / Annual Active
Reporting of Injuries, Diseases & Dangerous Occurrences Regs 2013 (RIDDOR) Certain workplace injuries, occupational diseases and dangerous occurrences must be reported to the HSE. Incident log with RIDDOR flag and follow-up action As required Active
Regulatory Reform (Fire Safety) Order 2005 Responsible person must carry out a fire risk assessment, maintain fire safety measures, train staff, and keep records. Fire RA, weekly fire safety checks, staff fire marshal training Annual Active
Control of Substances Hazardous to Health Regs 2002 (COSHH) Cleaning products and chemicals must be assessed, stored safely, and staff trained in their use. COSHH risk assessment, locked chemical store, staff training Annual Active
Manual Handling Operations Regs 1992 Avoid hazardous manual handling where reasonably practicable. Assess remaining risks, train staff. Manual handling RA, staff training records Annual Active
First Aid at Work Regs 1981 (amended 2013) Employer must provide adequate first aid facilities, trained first aiders, and an appointed person at all times. Trained first aider on every shift, stocked first aid kit 3-yearly cert Active
Electricity at Work Regs 1989 All electrical systems must be maintained to prevent danger. Portable appliances must be tested (PAT). EICR certificate, PAT testing, equipment checks Annual PAT Active
Workplace (Health, Safety & Welfare) Regs 1992 Premises must be maintained, ventilated, adequately lit, clean, with appropriate welfare facilities. Daily premises checks, hygiene log, maintenance log Daily / Ongoing Active
Health & Safety (Display Screen Equipment) Regs 1992 Workstation assessment for staff who regularly use computers or screens as part of their work. DSE assessment for admin/reception staff On change Active
Noise at Work Regs 2005 Assess noise levels in gym / group exercise. Take action if staff are regularly exposed above action levels. Volume limits in studio, RA for group exercise noise Annual Active
UK GDPR / Data Protection Act 2018 Member and staff personal data must be processed lawfully, stored securely, and retained only as long as necessary. Secure data systems, privacy notice, staff training Annual Active
Legionella — L8 ACOP (HSE) Duty to assess and control the risk of Legionella bacteria in hot and cold water systems. Written scheme required. Legionella risk assessment, water temperature checks Annual Active
⚠ This table is a reference guide. Always consult current HSE guidance and seek legal advice if unsure of your specific obligations. Legislation may be updated — check hse.gov.uk for the latest.
